Headway

Jobs to be done:

Gain knowledge from books faster

Who would be the product’s target persona?

Working professionals who want to capture as much knowledge as possible from books.

First Impression:

Looks very similar to Blinkist. Just curious if it has a better collection of books.

What works for me in this product:

  • Onboarding: The app asks for a bunch of questions to personalize the book suggestions that would come later. What is interesting it helps users in goal setting by asking “How much time are you willing to spend on books every day”. This, I think is a win-win for both the Headout and its users. The onboarding is otherwise step-bu-step and was not overwhelming for me
  • Personalization: It has a personalized feeds section whose intention is to engage its users. Otherwise rest of the personalization is pretty much a cookie-cutter experience.
  • Vale add Utility: One challenge with reading books is that over time you forget the details. To counter this Headway introduces the concept of Repetition. The insights that you had saved are presented back to you.

  • Gamification -
    • Reading streaks - You can track if you have been living up to the time commitment that you made at the time of onboarding.
    • Daily goals - Most users dont know what to start with when they begin with the product. To solve this issue, they have a everyday challenge where they reccomend one book for every single day in the area of your interest.

What does not work:

  • It is very hard to get a preview of the offering before going for a full purchase
  • UI feels a bit unrefined. e.g. The feeds section is non-apparent

Final Takeaway:

Looks like a very cool app with good value. However, I am a Blinkist subscriber, and hence would not want to buy Headway at this point of time.
